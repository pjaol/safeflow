## Gap Summary

### Clio Daye has parity with the market on
- Core period, symptom, mood, and notes logging
- Period, ovulation, and fertile window prediction
- Cycle history and irregular cycle support
- App lock / biometrics
- Dark mode

### Missing that most competitors have
- Cervical mucus and BBT logging
- Sexual activity and medication logging
- Apple Health sync
- Apple Watch app
- Home screen widget
- Pregnancy, TTC, and perimenopause modes
- Symptom pattern insights
- Doctor data export

### Things no competitor does well
- Natural Cycles has postpartum mode but no other app does
- Clue has the broadest wearable ecosystem (6+ devices) but it's premium-only
- GP Apps has the most languages (39+) but no cultural adaptation
